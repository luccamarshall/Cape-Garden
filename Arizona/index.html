About

At Cape Gardens Landscaping, we offer high-end design, construction, and maintenance services in the central Algarve area. With our award-winning team of highly experienced professionals based in the Quinta do Lago area, our dedication to excellence sets us apart.

With over 25 years of experience in the Algarve, we are client-focused, proactive, and professional, committed to producing and maintaining high-caliber schemes. Our dedication to creating stunning landscapes is reflected in our track record of accolades, awards, and satisfied clients. We take pride in our work and strive to bring out the beauty of every outdoor space we touch.

When you choose Cape Gardens Landscaping, you are choosing more than just a landscaping service. You are choosing a trusted partner who will bring your outdoor vision to life with precision and artistry.
