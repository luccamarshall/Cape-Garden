Projects

We combine mature plants, innovative layouts, and eco-friendly practices to deliver landscapes that make an immediate impact while prioritizing water-wise solutions. Our designs incorporate native plants and advanced irrigation systems to conserve water and thrive in the Algarve’s unique climate. From rejuvenating existing gardens to crafting new ones, we work closely with clients to deliver custom designs that reflect their vision and lifestyle.

What sets Cape Gardens apart is our client-focused approach, creative passion, and expert knowledge of the Algarve’s environment. Our team is dedicated to exceeding expectations, blending artistry and functionality to enhance your home’s architecture with beautiful, sustainable outdoor spaces. With a commitment to innovation and attention to detail, we ensure that each project not only stands out but also thrives in the local climate, providing lasting beauty and value.
