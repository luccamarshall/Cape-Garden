Maintenance

We offer professional garden maintenance services focused on creating sustainable, thriving green spaces. Our dedicated team provides regular garden maintenance, water-wise landscaping, seasonal planting, and tree care, using eco-friendly practices like organic fertilizers, composting, and natural pest control. We prioritize the use of native plants, promote biodiversity, and implement efficient irrigation systems to conserve water, ensuring your garden remains vibrant year-round.

Sustainability is at the core of our approach, with a commitment to preserving the natural beauty of the Algarve. Whether for villas, holiday homes, or commercial properties, Cape Gardens tailors its services to meet the unique needs of gardens in this Mediterranean climate. Contact us to create a beautiful, environmentally friendly garden that flourishes for years to come.
